About Wild Time Zoo

Wild Time Zoo has been a cornerstone of our community for 4 years, providing a unique blend of education, entertainment, and conservation. Our mission is to inspire a love for wildlife and promote environmental stewardship through immersive experiences and educational programs.

Our History

Founded in 2020, Wild Time Zoo started as a small wildlife sanctuary and has grown into a full-fledged zoological park. Over the years, we've expanded our facilities, increased our animal collection, and developed comprehensive educational programs to serve our community better.

Our Commitment

At Wild Time Zoo, we are committed to:

  • Safety: Ensuring the wellbeing of our animals, staff, and visitors is our top priority.
  • Education: Providing engaging and informative experiences that foster understanding and appreciation for wildlife.
  • Fun: Creating memorable experiences for visitors of all ages through interactive exhibits and exciting events.
  • Entertainment: Offering a diverse range of activities and attractions that delight and inspire our guests.

Conservation Efforts

We actively participate in conservation programs and collaborate with other zoos and wildlife organizations to protect endangered species and their habitats. Our breeding programs and research initiatives contribute to the global effort to preserve biodiversity.
