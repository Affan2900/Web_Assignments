'Hungry Lion' Picnic Areas

Unleash your inner carnivore at our six new 'Hungry Lion' picnic and barbeque areas strategically located throughout the zoo grounds. Each area is equipped with state-of-the-art grills, comfortable seating, and stunning views of nearby animal habitats. Whether you're grilling up a feast or enjoying a packed lunch, these scenic spots offer the perfect blend of nature and comfort for a memorable outdoor dining experience. Don't forget to roar with delight as you savor your meal!

New Gorilla Enclosure

Step into the lush world of our gentle giants in our brand new, state-of-the-art gorilla enclosure. This expansive habitat hosts our recently procured family of western lowland gorillas, providing them with a naturalistic environment that closely mimics their native African forests. Visitors can observe these magnificent primates from multiple viewing points, including an awe-inspiring canopy walkway.
